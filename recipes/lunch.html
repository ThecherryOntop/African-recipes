Beans and Yam Porridge

What is beans and yam porridge?

Beans and yam are some major foods in Nigeria. Beans is rich in protein and yam is a carbohydrate food. Cooking both foods together will results in some delicious nourishment. So let's get down to cooking.

Ingredients

  • Beans(You can use either bown or white)
  • Palm Oil
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Seasoning (Maggi)
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Yam
  • Fish and Meat (optional)
